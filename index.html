Milwaukee Soup

Menu

Welcome to Milwaukee Soup! My goal for this app is to offer a single place to see the daily soup offerings around Milwaukee. Right now, it shows Bella Caffe and The Soup House. I intend on adding The Soup Market next. Have a restaurant that you want to see added? Add a comment in the Google Play Store or email me at joe@steinbring.net.

Acknoledgements

This application was writen by Joe Steinbring, using PhoneGap Build and Kimono. The source code for this app is available via http://steinbring.net.

Would you like to get your restaurant's daily soup selection added to or removed from this application? Email joe@steinbring.net.

The soup bowl image on this app is courtesy of Charles Haynes (haynes), on flickr.
